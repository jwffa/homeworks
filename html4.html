Lionel Messi (born June 24, 1987, Rosario, Argentina) Argentine-born football (soccer) player who received a record-setting eight Ballon d'Or awards as the world's top male player (2009-12, 2015, 2019, 2021, and 2023). In 2022 he helped Argentina win the Fédération I nternationale de Football Association (FIFA)'s World Cup.

At age 13 Messi and his family relocated to Barcelona, and he began playing for FC Barcelona's under-14 team. He scored 21 goals in 14 games for the junior team, and he quickly graduated through the higher-level teams until at age 16 (on October 16, 2004) he was given his informal debut with FC Barcelona in a friendly match.
